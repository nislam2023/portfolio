04 : About me

Nazmul Islam is a seasoned Photonics Systems Engineer with a strong background in optical systems testing, analysis, and product development. With a Master of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering from McGill University and a Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Electronics from the Islamic University of Technology, Nazmul has honed his expertise in the field of photonics.

During his tenure at Ciena, Nazmul played a pivotal role in the development of optical line systems, actively participating in design and system architecture discussions. They excelled in Design Verification Testing (DVT), system testing, and data analysis, ensuring optimal performance and reliability of photonic systems. Their proficiency in automation, test management, and performance monitoring contributed to the successful delivery of high-quality products.

Prior to Ciena, He worked as a Senior Engineer at OZ Optics, where he designed and developed high-power fiber optic components and delivery systems. He also supervised team members and developed maintenance and operating standards for pilot projects.

Nazmul has a proven track record of collaborating with cross-functional teams, mentoring new hires, and managing lab equipment and projects. His core skills include optical systems testing and analysis, system integration, silicon photonics design, optical measurements, and programming in MATLAB and Python.

In his leisure time, he loves to spend time with his son, and loves to discover new places. Enjoying new cuisine is one of his favorites.
